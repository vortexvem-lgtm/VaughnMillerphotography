About me


Hi! My name is Vaughn, and I’m a photographer based in the Milwaukee area. I grew up with those little Tikes cameras where you could barely make out what the picture was. Back then, it wasn’t about sharpness or perfection — it was about curiosity and the instinct to capture something that felt important.


Now, I work with a camera capable of capturing moments that were never able to be seen before. You can feel the motion from the notion — the idea turning into movement — and the raw, down-low energy that lives inside each frame.


I focus on moody city scenes, quiet moments, and low-light storytelling. This space is a collection of moments that didn’t need to be loud. Whether it’s the intensity of motion or the stillness between it, my goal is to create images that feel lived in — moments you don’t just see, but feel.


Through light, shadow, and movement, my photography explores emotion, energy, and the subtle details that often go unnoticed.
